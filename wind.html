Windiness vs Latitude




This plot analyses the difference in wind speeds across the different humidity lines. Looking at the plot, wind speeds tended to be relatively low. Wind levels stayed below 16 knots in all areas, the greatest accumulation of wind speeds was below the 6 knot level. Even with this data, There was nothing to suggest that there was any correlation between wind speeds and latitude locations.
